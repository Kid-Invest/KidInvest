Account Balance

Your account balance is the amount of money you have after adding all your business and stock earnings and subtracting all your purchases. You will begin with $10,000 and may use this money to buy inventory, addons, and stock. You will also be able to earn $500 for every correct answer on your quizzes!

Profit

Profit is the amount of money you earned after you subtract the amount of money you spent on buying inventory and operating your business. For example, if the cost of lemonade ingredients is $1 and you sell each lemonade for $4, then you make a profit of $3.

Inventory

Inventory is a complete list of the items you have available to make lemonades. For example, you need lemons, sugar, water, ice, and other fruits to make your lemonades. When you are running out of any one of these items, you must purchase more to add to your inventory so you can make more lemonades.

Transaction

A transaction is when you buy or sell something. Each time you buy inventory, this will be shown in your transactions page. Your daily sales will be shown in your sales transactions page.

Popularity

The more popular your lemonade stand is, the more your customers will be willing to pay to try your lemonade. Higher prices means more profit, and more profit means more money!

Addons

In this game, purchasing addons will increase the popularity of your lemonade stand. For example, if you purchase a radio, more customers will enjoy visiting your lemonade stand and will be more likely to purchase lemonades.

Invest

To invest is to put money, time or effort into something to make a profit or gain an advantage. You can invest time and money into your lemonade business and invest money into stocks that may increase in value over time.

Stock

A stock is a type of investment that represents an ownership piece or share of a company. Investors buy stocks that they think will go up in value over time and increase their profits. However, some companies lose value or go out of business and investors may lose part or all of their investment. This is why it is important to buy stock in different companies instead of focusing on just one.

Market Price

A stockâ€™s market price is the price that it sells for at a given point in time. The market price increases and decreases throughout the trading day as investors buy and sell stocks. The market prices rises if more people want to buy it and will fall as people begin to sell more of the stock.

Profiting From Your Stocks

In this game, you can profit from buying shares and selling them when their current market price is higher than when you bought them. This is what it means to "buy low and sell high"

Total Return

The total return is a measure of an investments overall performance and growth over time. So if you buy shares and their value increases since you bought them, the difference will be your total return.

Investment Portfolio

An investment portfolio is a grouping of financial assets. In this case, your portfolio includes your stocks and your business profits.
