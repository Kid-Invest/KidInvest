A little bit about Kid Invest

Kid Invest is a learning platform targeted towards 7+ years old youth. This investing and business simulator will teach kids to learn, play, and practice investing at an early age. Kids will gain financial literacy to become a successful money-maker. The lemonade stand will operate like a regular company where users will need to purchase supplies, expand the store, and sell to customers. With earnings, kids will invest in kid-friendly companies created using real-time stock market data where the businesses simulate actual stock fluctuations.
